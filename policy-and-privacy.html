Privacy Policy | Classroomgame.github.io

We may collect both personal and non-personal information through the following means:

  • Automatically collected data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed.

  • Cookies and tracking technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze trends, and deliver relevant ads.

  • User-submitted content: If you comment or interact with features on our site, we may collect the content you submit.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To monitor usage and analyze trends.

  • To ensure compliance with legal requirements and terms of use.

3. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to:

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect certain features of the website.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand user behavior and traffic trends.

These services may collect information directly from your browser and use cookies as described in their respective privacy policies.

Our website is accessible to all ages but is not intended to collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us, and we will delete it promptly.

6. User-Generated Content

When users submit comments or other content, it may be publicly visible. Please avoid sharing personal or sensitive information in public forums.

7. Data Security

We implement basic security measures to protect the information collected. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Please contact us through the Website if you wish to exercise any of these rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

We do not maintain a dedicated email address. Please contact us via the comment or feedback features available on the Website.
